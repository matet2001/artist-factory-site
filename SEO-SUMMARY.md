## 🎓 How It Works - SEO Breakdown

### When someone searches "próbaterem budapest":
1. **Google crawls** your sitemap.xml
2. **Reads metadata** from layout.tsx (title, description, keywords)
3. **Understands content** from structured data (you're a MusicVenue in Budapest)
4. **Sees location** in LocalBusiness schema (Pozsonyi út 13)
5. **Checks relevance** based on keywords in content
6. **Ranks your site** based on:
   - Relevance (✅ optimized)
   - Authority (build backlinks)
   - User experience (✅ fast, mobile-friendly)
   - Local signals (set up Google Business Profile)
